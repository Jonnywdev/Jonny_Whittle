most Recent Project

Leigh Festival - The New Leeds

Visit the website Leighfestival.uk

Leigh Festival was created by me and a friend, we are both on the committee at Leigh RUFC, where the event was held. We wanted to host a fun event for the whole community. We had Live music, from local bands, singles and DJ's. A mixed ability rugby match, sevens tournament, local food vans and a beer festival.

My Key Responsibilities

- Create a simple to use website people could use to find all information about the festival.

- Manage a team of volunteers in planning and throughout the weekend.

- Create and manage social media and printed artwork e.g. Banners.

We are now working on making next year even bigger!
