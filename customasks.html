Customasks

I realised that masks were going to stick around for a while so I decided I would try and make a little bit of money from them, using my design skills to create fun custom masks for people.

I decided to make my own company and contact a supplier so that I could design people their own custom masks. My main objective was to create a company which could give people the design they wanted on their mask without having to spend too much money. I also wanted the masks to be high quality. After many tests I found my right supplier, Contrado.
